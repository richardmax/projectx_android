WHAT DO THE LED COLOURS INDICATE?

Green, steady: Your Upp is powered on and charging.

Green, breathing: Your Upp cartridge is nearing the end of its life and will require replacing soon.

Green, blinking: Programming of your Upp device is in progress.

Orange, steady: The Upp charger needs to be updated.  Please connect the dust cap adapter and make connection via USB cable to a laptop or PC.

Orange, breathing: Your Upp device has detected low fuel level within the fuel cartridge. Please replace fuel cartridge.

Red, steady: Your Upp fuel cartridge can not be authenticated.  Upp will shut down.

Red, blinking: The firmware image could not be recognised.

Black: Off
