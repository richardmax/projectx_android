YOUR Upp™ HAS SHUT DOWN UNEXPECTEDLY

Upp will automatically shut down if it detects your device is fully charged.  This will protect the internal battery of your device and extend its life.

Upp will shutdown if it detects poor airflow.  The fuel cell requires a sufficient flow of oxygen to properly operate.

Upp will shut down when it detects a low fuel level in the cartridge.  Please restart Upp and check the LED indicator light.
